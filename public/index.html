About Hesiod Financial

We are a hedge fund managed by a collective of Case Western Reserve University, Princeton University, and Colorado College students with distinct and unique views of macro-economic trends. It is our belief that through strong and careful quantitative analysis of global markets, we can take advantage of underlying shifts in the economy through a variety of financial instruments including equity, futures, options, and cryptocurrencies.

We believe that from our vantage point with strong technical knowledge in emerging technologies, including cryptocurrencies, we can beat the market with an aggressive-focused portfolio, diversified to weather through even down markets.
